Research Projects

My research interests are in the domain of log analytics , machine learning and data mining. Currently i am working on Anomaly detection from Event Log Empiricism.

In this project we aim to detect anomaly from a large scale systems using EMS (Event Message System) log . Also we plan to develop an automated early warning system to protect the system from failure.
