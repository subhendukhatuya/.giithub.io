Research Projects

My research interests are in the domain of NLP, AI, CV, and deep learning, log analytics, anomaly detection. During my MS tenure, I have worked on Anomaly detection from Event Log Empiricism.

In this project we aim to detect anomaly from a large scale systems using EMS (Event Message System) log . Also developed an automated early warning system to protect the system from failure.
